Welcome

The PLUREL project: Peri-urban Land Use Relationships - Strategies and Sustainability Assessment Tools for Urban-Rural Linkages is a European integrated research project within the European Commissions sixth framework programme. The project began on the first of January 2007 and ended 31st of March 2011.
